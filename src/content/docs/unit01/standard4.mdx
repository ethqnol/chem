## Converting To Scientific Notation

Scientific notation is the representation of a measurement in this form:

$$ N * 10^n $$

Where N is between 1 and 10 and "n" represents how many times a number must be multiplied by 10 to reach the number in it's non-scientific notation. For example, the number 260 would be represented as:

$2.6 * 10^2$.
